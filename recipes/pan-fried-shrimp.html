Pan-Fried Shrimp

Description

Prepare tasty shrimp on the stovetop to serve as a quick, protein-packed meal for your kids

Directions

  • 8 tbsp butter
  • 1 pound peeled shrimp
  • 1 tbsp lemon
  • 1 pinch garlic salt
  • Salt and pepper for seasoning
Steps
  1. Melt 6 tbsp butter in large skillet
  2. Add shrimp and cook for 2 minutes
  3. Reduce heat to medium-low
  4. Stir 1.5 tsps lemon juice, garlic salt, salt and pepper
  5. Cook until heated through, approx 1 minute
  6. Remove from heat and stir in reamining 2 tbsps butter and 1.5 tsps lemon juice
  7. Serve

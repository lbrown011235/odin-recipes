Air Fry Baked Potatoes

Description

This quick-and-easy recipe only requires three ingredients and 15 minutes. It results in a perfect light bite or meal replacement

Ingredients

  • 2 large potatoes
  • 1 tbsp peanut oil
  • 0.5 tsp sea salt
Steps
  1. Brush potatoes with sea salt and oil
  2. Cook potatoes in air fryer until tender, approx 1 hour
  3. Serve and enjoy

The Big Lasagna

Description

This decadent lasagna is made of handmade pasta layered with rich bechamel, savory tomato sauce, and creamy ricotta filling. Regardless of how you garnish this dish, the final product is enough to satiate a family of four.

Ingredients

  • 4.5 cups of tomato sauce
  • 16 fresh store bought lasagna sheets
  • 1.5 oz grated Parmesan
  • 3 oz whole milk
Steps
  1. Prepare the ricotta filling
  2. Combine mozzarella, Parmesan, basil, and salt in large bowl
  3. Prepare the bechamel
  4. Prepare the lasagna
  5. Add the fillings to the lasagna
  6. Bake for 1 hour
  7. Cool for 15 minutes and serve
